Most webpage documents contain content at the top and bottom of all pages. The header and footer elements do not provide functionality in themselves, however they enable the ability to define content in the header and footer.

Often a webpage header contains something like a logo or banner or even an navigation menu.

A header element is not limited only to the start of a page It can declare a header to any area of the webpage like a section or a article. The header element is intended to hold H1-H6, however you can populate it with anything needed.
